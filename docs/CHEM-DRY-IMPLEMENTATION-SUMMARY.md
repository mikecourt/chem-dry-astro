## üîí **Brand Compliance Checklist**

### **‚úÖ Required Messaging**
- ‚úÖ "uses **about** 80% less water" (never without "about")
- ‚úÖ "no **dirt-attracting** residue" (never just "no residue")
- ‚úÖ "The process used by Chem-Dry" (never "Chem-Dry's process")
- ‚úÖ "**The Natural¬Æ**" (proper capitalization + ¬Æ)

### **‚ùå Prohibited Phrases**
- ‚ùå "Chem-Dry's" (possessive form)
- ‚ùå "all natural" or "green certified"
- ‚ùå "no residue" (without "dirt-attracting")
- ‚ùå "uses 80% less water" (without "about")
